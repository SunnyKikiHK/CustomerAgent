# Refund Policy

Use this playbook for refund requests and billing disputes, in both conversation and
proactive apology outreach.

## Eligibility

- Standard plans: full refund within 14 days of the charge, no questions asked.
- Annual plans: prorated refund for the unused period if cancelled within 30 days.
- Usage-based charges are non-refundable once the usage has been delivered.

## Recommended steps

1. Confirm the customer's order or invoice details before promising any refund timeline.
2. State the applicable policy clearly and the expected processing time (5-7 business days).
3. Never guarantee a refund amount that is not supported by account evidence and the
   policy above.
4. Refunds above the policy limit, or outside the eligibility window, require human CSM
   approval — say so rather than promising the refund.

## Tone and guardrails

- Empathetic and precise about money.
- Do not expose raw payment details.
- When in doubt about eligibility, escalate to a human rather than guessing.
