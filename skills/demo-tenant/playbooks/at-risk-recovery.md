# At-Risk Customer Recovery

Use this playbook when a customer's health score is low or their product usage has
dropped sharply. The goal is to re-engage the customer before they churn.

## When to trigger

- Health score below 50, or
- Weekly active usage down more than 30% week-over-week, or
- A support-ticket spike combined with an upcoming renewal.

## Recommended steps

1. Review the customer's recent usage trend and open support tickets before reaching out.
2. Send a personalized, empathetic check-in email from the assigned CSM. Reference the
   specific feature or workflow where usage dropped — never a generic "just checking in".
3. Offer concrete help: a tailored onboarding session, a relevant integration guide, or a
   short call to remove the current blocker.
4. If there is no reply within 48 hours, escalate by notifying the assigned CSM on Slack
   with full context.

## Tone and guardrails

- Be specific and helpful, not salesy.
- Never promise a discount, credit, or roadmap commitment that is not pre-approved.
- If the customer is clearly frustrated, acknowledge it and set the expectation that a
  human CSM will follow up.
